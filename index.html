  • Seat Upholstery

    We specialize in seatupholstery for your car, truck, boat, motorcycle, airplane, snow mobile,golf cart, wave runner, and bus. From inserts to complete seat coverswe offer a variety of colors and materials including leather, vinyl, cloth,suede, ostrich, sting ray, and snake skin. We specialize in theconversion from cloth to leather seat covers using factory OEM colors. We repair, repad and replace foam. We can also weld brokenseat frames and repair your broken spring bed assembly.

  • Convertible Tops

    We can replace theconvertible, simulated or vinyl top for your original, antique or musclecar with a wide variety of colors in vinyl or canvas. Is the backwindow of your convertible top broken or is it separating from the convertibletop material? In some cases we can change just the rear window assemblywithout replacing the entire top. The brands of convertible topswe install include: Robbins, GAHH, Easy-On, and Auto Tops. We alsorepair and replace canvas boat tops, plastic windows, and marine zippersand snaps. We use Sunbrella fabric in a wide variety of colors.

You can be sure they will see you coming when we install your LEDor HID lighting, light bar and Oracle lights.
